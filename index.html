Why you should come to see us

At Wild Circus, we believe society is at th earliest stages of learning how what we do for a living both threatens nature and fails to meet our deepest human needs

We believe Humans are ingenious, adaptive and clever. We design shows to contribute to this learning and to (re)explore our heritage, our environment. Our unique know-how is sourcing its talent at the venue itself to create a show meaningful based on the culture, the history and the talents.

The Show is design in a way that it is adapted to every public. Children will find entertainment and elders will find a spiritual ans welcome message that can be awake some vocations.
