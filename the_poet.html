Who is the Poet

Her stage name is Soko, she has a melancolic singing style that she agreed to use to recall us all our responsabilities

If you don't like hearing you are wrong, don't come to this show, Soko will use her sarcasm in her text to depict our lifestyle, Her text are full of humor to be loved by the children, but the real meaning of it can be groundshaking for adults.

Her show will always come last to calm down your excited kids and to give a last moment to reflection.
